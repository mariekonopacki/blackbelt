From Newcomer to Market Leader

Having maxed out SEO strategies, NerdWallet sought a new approach to generate brand awareness to drive traffic. With Blackbelt’s partnership, NerdWallet did just that. Together, we successfully positioned NerdWallet as a market leader, moving from #4 to #1 amongst competitors.

We used our data-first approach to test messaging, validate audience segmentation and confidently scale, rather than blindly rush into mass-market communication. We internalized NerdWallet’s market segmentation, persona definition, and customer journey, and built upon this to deploy a systematic content creation and distribution approach for online and TV. We then were able to iterate on the lessons learned in the least expensive channels and take those learnings into tests on additional channels and ultimately on national DRTV as a means to launch the new NerdWallet brand.

Let’s get really personal

Blackbelt was tasked to help AT&T achieve a 20% reduction in call volume to their service centers at times when their customers go through routine upgrades and service installations. They achieved this goal by employing personalized video to give their consumers the information and confidence they need.

With our partners at Infosys, we developed a video personalization engine and content plan that creates on-demand unique content for each consumer. Employing a modular approach to content, we leveraged AT&T’s brand assets to build components to fuel the engine.

Through machine learning, these videos can evolve and become more effective at reducing call volume over time as they learn the habits and behaviors of types of customers for increased personalization.

Disrupting the Data Center... Again

The mere idea of network virtualization seems an oxymoron when VMWare brought this product innovation to the data centers. Blackbelt enabled VMware to better position the value propositions for their ground-breaking Network Virtualization product suite to an entirely new audience that trades in the physical world of servers and switches?

A better understanding of how to gradually unwrite generations of legacy instincts and perceptions could come from none other than the customers themselves. Through boots-on-the-ground ethnographic research and a thorough customer-focused assessment across all touchpoints of the journey, Blackbelt built a fact-based strategy for how VMware can best resonate both their content and experiences to an audience that is often hard-to-reach.
